## LinkedIn Version

I am building XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal: an education-first onboarding, partner discovery and proof layer for the XRPL ecosystem.

The idea is simple: users should learn and understand a route before they take action.

The MVP includes:

- Partner Hub for education-first discovery
- Xaman payload creation
- XRPL transaction verification
- optional on-ledger Proof Stamps
- local XP and Reward Ledger
- Truth Desk service route
- Access Gate payment route
- Pitch Mode and Submission Pack

The project is intentionally non-custodial and compliance-aware. It does not act as broker, does not provide yield and does not execute trades. It explains, routes and verifies proof.
