## Tagline

Education-first XRPL onboarding, partner discovery and proof layer.

## One-Liner

XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal helps users learn first, understand risk, connect safely and verify meaningful XRPL actions with SourceTag `2606170002`.

## Short Description

XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal is an education-first onboarding, partner discovery and proof layer for XRPL users, builders and service routes.

The MVP combines learning flows, partner route checklists, Xaman payload creation, optional on-ledger Proof Stamps, XRPL transaction verification, local XP, Truth Desk service routes and Access Gate payment routes.

The Terminal does not custody funds, does not act as broker, does not provide yield and does not execute trades. It explains routes, shows risk awareness and directs users to official providers after education.

## Long Description

XRPL has many powerful tools, but new users often meet them as separate doors: wallets, onramps, DEX routes, Xahau, bridges, partner apps and DeFi concepts. Without a guided education path, users may take high-risk actions before they understand what is happening.

XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal solves this by turning XRPL onboarding into a guided hallway. Users learn first, complete risk-awareness steps, connect wallet when needed, create Xaman payloads, verify transaction hashes and record progress through local XP or optional on-ledger Proof Stamps.

For builders and partners, the Terminal works as a discovery and explanation layer. Partner routes can be shown with simple education cards, risk notes, official links and optional proof verification. For users, the experience is safer and more structured. For the ecosystem, it creates a repeatable way to onboard users without pretending to be an exchange, custodian or financial advisor.

The Make Waves proof identity uses SourceTag `2606170002`.

## Problem

XRPL has strong infrastructure, but onboarding can feel fragmented. New users see many different tools and concepts without a clear order:

- wallets
- onramps
- DEX routes
- bridges
- Xahau
- partner apps
- issued currencies
- DeFi concepts
- NFTs
- proof systems

This creates confusion, risk and low conversion for builders.

## Solution

XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal gives users a clear route:

1. Learn the route.
2. Read risk notes.
3. Visit official provider links.
4. Connect wallet only when needed.
5. Create Xaman payloads.
6. Verify XRPL transaction hashes.
7. Record progress through XP or Proof Stamps.

## Why SourceTag Matters

The SourceTag gives the project a recognizable XRPL proof marker. It lets the Terminal verify that a transaction belongs to the correct route, without creating unnecessary transactions for every click.

Clicks and reading steps stay off-chain as local XP. Meaningful completed routes can optionally create an on-ledger Proof Stamp.
