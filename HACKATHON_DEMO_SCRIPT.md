# XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal — 2 Minute Demo Script

## Demo Goal

Show that XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal is not just another wallet dashboard. It is an education-first onboarding, partner discovery and proof layer for XRPL users, builders and service routes.

## Exact Safe Opening Line

XRPL OnTheTrack Terminal is education-first. It does not custody funds, does not act as broker, does not provide yield and does not execute trades. It explains XRPL routes, shows risk awareness and directs users to official providers.
